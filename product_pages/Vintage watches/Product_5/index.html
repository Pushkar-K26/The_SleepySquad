A Legacy

The Cellini 4017 Midas is a watch with royal lineage — worn by legends, admired by collectors, and created during Rolex’s golden design era.

Named after the famed Italian sculptor Benvenuto Cellini and inspired by King Midas, this watch was part of an exclusive production line in the 1970s. Its unique left-sided crown, solid gold body, and bold geometry set it apart from conventional designs.

A blend of daring creativity and horological mastery, the Cellini 4017 Midas reflects a time when Rolex redefined luxury not only through engineering, but by creating wearable art.
