A Legacy

The Lord Elgin A-11 is a cornerstone of vintage military watch design, issued to U.S. forces during WWII and renowned for its durability and precision. Designed under rigorous wartime standards, it offered reliable timekeeping in combat zones and harsh conditions.

Elgin, a historic American watchmaker, crafted the A-11 with simplicity, legibility, and toughness at its core—qualities that shaped it into a timeless design icon. Its black dial, luminous hands, and hand-wound movement remain iconic to this day.

Today, the A-11 is more than a military artifact. It represents resilience, craftsmanship, and a pivotal chapter in horological and world history.
