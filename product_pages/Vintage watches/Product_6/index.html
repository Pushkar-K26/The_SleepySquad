A Legacy

The Calatrava line, born in the 1930s, stands as the purest expression of Patek Philippe’s philosophy: understated elegance, uncompromising quality, and horological excellence.

The 2598 is a rare gem within this lineage — blending minimalist Bauhaus aesthetics with mid-century refinement. Originally designed as a gentleman’s dress watch, its discreet form and manual movement exude classic watchmaking appeal.

A relic of the golden age of mechanical watches, the Calatrava 2598 continues to inspire generations of collectors and craftsmen alike.
