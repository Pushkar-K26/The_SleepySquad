Jacob & Co

Jacob & Co. is not confined by the past — it challenges convention and dares to redefine luxury with every creation. Uniting radical design with uncompromising craftsmanship, the brand forges a new path in haute horlogerie where bold imagination meets technical brilliance. Each timepiece is a masterpiece of innovation, a symbol of fearlessness, and a celebration of visionary artistry.

Rolex

For over a century, Rolex has fused Swiss precision with enduring prestige. Established in 1905, the brand has consistently set the benchmark for excellence in watchmaking. From pioneering the first waterproof wristwatch to mastering the complexities of mechanical innovation, Rolex is not just a brand — it's a legacy etched in time.
